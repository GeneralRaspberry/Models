### Mechanistic Spatial SIS Model

A spatially explicit stochastic SIS epidemic model is implemented on a two-dimensional domain with toroidal topology, ensuring no boundary effects.

Individuals are introduced as either susceptible (grey) or infected (red) and move continuously throughout the environment. Transmission emerges mechanistically through proximal contact between hosts rather than being imposed through a mass-action assumption i.e. infectious status does not influence transmission dynamics.
